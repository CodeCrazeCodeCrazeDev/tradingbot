The goal: actively and continuously attack every live signal, every candidate edge, every assumption. This is not a one‑time validation step; it is a permanent adversarial process that lives alongside production.

Role‑based access (RBAC) with tiered privilege:

Data librarian: can manage data ingestion, but not view models or strategies.

Execution operator: monitors P&L, risk, kill‑switch; cannot modify models or access raw signal code.

Segregation of duties: The person who authors a signal must never be the one who approves it or who monitors its live performance. All promotions require two‑person integrity (digitally attested).

3. Model Signing & Integrity
Every model artifact is:

Serialized with a deterministic hash of its architecture + trained weights.

Signed by the author’s private key (attached to their physical YubiKey or HSM).

Counter‑signed by the staging validation service after all counterintelligence tests pass.

The production bootloader refuses any artifact that isn’t counter‑signed and whose data‑feed manifests aren’t all compliant (timestamp, license, provenance).

4. Immutable Audit & Kill‑Switch
Immutable audit logs (write‑once, append‑only ledger) record every signal promotion, counterintelligence attack result, data‑license violation, and model execution path.

Kill‑switch: a dedicated, physically separate hardware button (or cloud‑based equivalent with multi‑party authorization) that instantly liquidates all positions and revokes all model execution tokens. The kill‑switch cannot be overridden by any automated process. It is tested monthly.

5. Data Provenance & Compliance Scoring Enforced at Every Hop
Every data record (tick, filing, news article) carries:

Origin UUID, license fingerprint, acquisition timestamp, compliance score (1–5).

The Source Validation Layer assigns a score and rejects any data that cannot prove its lineage. If a news vendor’s license expires, the feed is automatically quarantined mid‑stream, and any signal depending on it is suspended within seconds.

Counterintelligence tests specifically measure how a signal behaves when its lowest‑compliance sources are perturbed.

6. No Live Self‑Modification
AlphaAlgo models cannot write to their own weight files or hyperparameters in production. Any parameter update must go through the full staging + counterintelligence cycle as a new model artifact.

Performance feedback (Layer 8) only goes back to a research read‑only database that is disconnected from live execution. This prevents runaway feedback loops.
