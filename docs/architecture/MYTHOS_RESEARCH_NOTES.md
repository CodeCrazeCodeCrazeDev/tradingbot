# Mythos Research Notes for AlphaAlgo

Research date: 2026-04-25

## Reliable Findings

Anthropic describes Claude Mythos Preview as a general-purpose frontier model with advanced agentic coding and reasoning skills, released only to limited Project Glasswing partners for defensive cybersecurity work. The official Project Glasswing announcement says the model found many previously unknown vulnerabilities in major operating systems, browsers, and critical software, and that Anthropic does not plan to make it generally available.

The Frontier Red Team writeup emphasizes that most discovered vulnerabilities were still unpatched at publication time, so detailed disclosure was intentionally withheld under coordinated vulnerability disclosure. This is the most important operational lesson for AlphaAlgo: strong bug-finding capability must be paired with strict output controls, audit, patch-first workflows, and human/security review before disclosure.

Anthropic's transparency page states that Mythos Preview is being deployed with limited access, real-time classifier guards, and access controls. It also says Mythos was trained with synthetic data generated by other models, but the architecture and training recipe remain proprietary. Public recurrent-depth transformer claims should therefore be treated as hypothesis, not fact.

## AlphaAlgo Design Translation

- Use Mythos-class reasoning for defensive security, code hardening, trading research, and post-trade analysis, not for autonomous live execution.
- Prefer real-world validation tasks and regression tests over benchmark-only confidence.
- Run untrusted strategy code, generated patches, unknown files, and external repo experiments inside ephemeral sandbox sessions with no persistent secrets and no direct production access.
- Gate live deployment, broker credential changes, risk-limit changes, capital scaling, production patching, strategy promotion, and kill-switch override with signed approval.
- Keep vulnerability details private until patched or maintainers approve coordinated disclosure.
- Route work through persistent specialist agents: risk, execution, macro, backtest, code review, and security.
- Use sealed provenance traces and append-only audit logs for risky decisions.

## Implemented Hooks

- `MythosInspiredAIGovernor` converts research lessons into contained task plans.
- `GlassBoxOverseer` emits action classification, risk category, policy rule, required evidence, and missing evidence.
- `TieredSandboxMesh` creates ephemeral sandbox specs for risky actions.
- `ToolProxyVault` issues scoped tokens without exposing long-lived secrets.
- `StrictPatchApprovalPipeline` creates patch proposals and bugfix workflow reports without applying them.
- `FormalTradingInvariantRegistry` checks hard safety invariants rather than market predictions.
- `DebatePostTrainingValidator` applies self-consistency and debate validation to trade thesis validation, strategy selection, post-trade diagnosis, backtest interpretation, and research ranking.
